What is this Website?

A horrible but hopefully entertaining attempt at trying to stay on topic while explaining Guilty Gear.

This music video was made to celebrate the first anniversary of Guilty Gear: -Strive-, the latest entry in the franchise. The visuals depict certain important scenes in the incredibly long and complex story of the series while the music is the theme of Sol Badguy, the protagonist, form the latest entry. In Guilty Gear: -Strive-, each playable character has their own dedicated vocal theme that provides insight into their life, experiences, and views. While I do personally prefer the vocal tracks in Guilty Gear Xrd over those in Strive, it is a very effective approach to ensure that players will have some understanding of the characters and their story even when just getting into the series.
